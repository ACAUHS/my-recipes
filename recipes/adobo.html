Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description

Chicken adobo is a personal favourite of many Filipinos, including myself. It is a fairly simple recipe, and does not take too long to prepare. For todays recipe, we will be making chicken adobo, but if you were going to make pork adobo, just follow the same recipe, substituting the chicken for the pork.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 3-4 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1-2 bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3-4 black peppercorn

Steps

  1. Wash chicken from packaging and place in medium-large sized bowl
  2. Add all ingredients in bowl
  3. Let chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes (max 2 hours)
  4. Take chicken out and sear both sides on medium heat
  5. Pour marinade and boil until mix becomes saucy
  6. OPTIONAL: Add 1/2 cup of water and let it boil until saucy
