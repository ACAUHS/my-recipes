Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

This is probably the most simple recipe you could ever make in your own house. All that is needed are your desired number of eggs, a bit of milk, salt-pepper, and optionally cheese!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Splash of milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Crack your desired number of eggs into a bowl
  2. Scramble eggs using a fork
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Add butter to a pan on medium heat
  5. Add eggs to the pan and constantly move around
  6. Once eggs are cooked add cheese and mix
  7. Add to plate
